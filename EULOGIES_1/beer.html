DESCRIPTION
MEMORY
An empty can of Coors Light.

Dents appear around the can.
My parents were so anal about me drinking while underage (rightfully so). One summer my parents had a cookout and there it was: an ice bucket filled with cans of Coors Light. As everyone was preoccupied with conversation, I saw that as my opportunity. I WANTED BEER! Sweat was dripping. Chest was thumping. Eyes were focused on that bucket. In a quick second, I beeline'd to ice bucket and snatched a can. I locked my bed room door, downed the beer, and tossed it in my desk. It has been in there for about 4 years.
