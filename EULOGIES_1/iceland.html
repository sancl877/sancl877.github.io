MEMORY
A manila envelope containing a photograph of a winter in Reykjavík.
My first trip without my family was in January of 2017. I left New York with three of my friends to Reykjavík, Iceland. On our first day in the city, we walked around Reykjavík and I spotted this dainty camera shop. I already pre-planned the things I wanted to bring back to the States and a photograph from an Icelandic photographer was one of them. Among the sea of beautiful photographs, you stood out to me. You were strong in contrast to how I saw Iceland to be on the Internet. Everyone seemed to portray Iceland as being a light and heavenly place, but you provided a new perspective for me. You exuded an eerie quality that I was so drawn to. You were freeing but dangerous at the same time. You stay hanging on the wall of my room. The one with the only window.
