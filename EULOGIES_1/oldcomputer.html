MEMORY
A rusty, dusty, crusty Toshiba slapped with an apple sticker.
After getting caught watching porn on my aunt's computer, my parents decided that I needed to get my own. I forget when I first got you, but here are some facts. First and foremost, I wanted a Mac. I was so frustrated with you that I decided to stick an Apple sticker I got from my iPod nano right on your face. And no, it was not meant to be ironic. Despite my intial reaction of not being so nice to you, I grew to appreciate what you have done for me. You were a big part in exploring how art and technology molds together. You carry so many files of my earliest computer art works created using torrented versions of Paint Tool Sai, Sony Vegas Pro, and Photoshop. Whenever I see you, there is this rush of nostalgia that overcomes me.

P.S. I'm sorry I watched 2 Girls 1 Cup on you.
