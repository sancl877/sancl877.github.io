Warning: this particular object is incredibly embarrassing for me to write about. Here it is:

I am going to try to dilute this story of 3 years into one short paragraph. I can't pinpoint the exact moment that I heard of One Direction, but I do remember seeing Zayn Malik and crying. After that, I made social medias dedicated to all of them. From then on, I have built numerous friendships over our bond for this boy band.

During the summer of 2013, One Direction announced their tour and they were playing 30 minutes away from me. My parents blessed me with tickets, but under one circumstance: bring my uncle. Literally imagine being a young boy with his grown ass uncle who did not give a fuck about One Direction listening to One Direction for 3 hours straight. So there I was, literally crying living my best gay life while my uncle sat on the bleacher looking so miserable.
