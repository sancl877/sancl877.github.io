An assortment of brushes
You guys were a collection of over 4 years of painting. Watercolor painting acted a form of therapy for me. The spontaneous nature of the medium was so beautiful and organic, that I played around with it every single day of high school.

I feel as though I have neglected you. I never paint anymore. I never find the time to. I miss you guys. Maybe we will see each other in the near future.
