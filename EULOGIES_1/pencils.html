A set of carved sticks that also act as pencils. They are bound by red and white thread.
After I graduated, my high school art teacher invited all the AP Art students over to her house in Long Island. When we got there, she insisted we walk around the fair happening in the town over. As we were walking, I found this vintage antique store. Among the items, I found you and was intrigued. There is no practical use for you though.
