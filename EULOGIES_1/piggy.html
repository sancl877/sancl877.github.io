× EULOGY FOR THE
PIGGY BANK
DESCRIPTION
MEMORY
A very heavy blue piggy bank filled to the top with coins and dollars. There seems to be no way to open the piggy bank.
Me and my mother brought you home from a Chinese grocery store in Queens, NY. Ever since then, my mother forcefully put any money I gathered into the piggy bank. What I did not realize was that you had no way for me to open you up. I literally cannot retrieve the money out of you. That is why I left you. You never opened up to me.
