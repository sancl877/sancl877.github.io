A VHS tape containing film of the Japanese animation, Sailor Moon.
One of my biggest guilty pleasures is my love for Sailor Moon. My mom picked up the sailor moon VHS when I was just a child. She would run the tape every single day before I went to bed. I don't remember a single fucking thing about the show other than the catchy theme song and the fierce outfits of Sailor Moon and the Sailor Scouts. They really fucking killed me with those looks. Also, the theme song is literally engrained in my brain.
