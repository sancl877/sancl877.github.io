MEMORY
A 3-disc set of the show "South Park."
There was two shows as a child that I fell in love with, Drawn Together and South Park. Shocker. At the time, I could only watch those shows when they were on TV. My smart ass thought "If I had a DVD of these shows, I could watch them whenever and wherever!" I finessed my way into getting my parents to drive me to BestBuy in order to get some DVDs. I picked up the South Park DVD set and my mom asked me, "What is this?"
"Oh, it's a cartoon on Disney." I replied.
"Okay!" my mom said.

Now you are mine.
