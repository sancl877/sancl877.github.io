TOGA
DESCRIPTION
MEMORY
A white outer garment made of cotton. The piece of cloth is draped along the chest and arms.
As I was growing up in the Church, I was forced to become a choir member. Alongside the HYMNAL BOOK, the toga was part of the choir uniform. The toga was a symbol of purity. However, my relationship with the toga is heavily flawed. I was forced to wear this symbol although I was accepting of how unpure I was.
