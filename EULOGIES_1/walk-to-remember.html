A DVD of "A Walk to Remember"
My mom was in love with you. Whenever she buys her own DVD of a specific movie, that is a tell tale sign that movie had a significant impact on her.

You were on almost every other day, but as a kid, I don't remember much. I vividly remember some scenes such as one of Landon's friends jumping off a cliff into the lake scene and Landon and Jamie's on screen kiss. Despite my lack of understanding the movie, it always a go to for me. I think I was drawn to the body language of Landon and Jamie. That in itself allowed me to design a narrative in my head.

About 4 years ago, I decided to read the book you were based off of and it all made sense.
