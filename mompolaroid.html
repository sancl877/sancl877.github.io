A Polaroid Captiva SLR that seems to no longer work. In the frame holder is a polaroid of me.

I discovered a polaroid of me in the frame holder and my immediate thought was that I looked fucking great! Upon further research on the Internet, no one still manufactures the film for this specific camera. I really wish I could you soon.
