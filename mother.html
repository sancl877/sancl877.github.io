I think leaving you was the hardest pill to swallow when I left for school. The feelings were mutual when you got worried that I'd be on my own. There has been countless of times when I missed your cooking, your smell, your voice.

Although you were never so vocal about it, you were very accepting of how I presented myself. You accepted my want for independence and my flamboyant self expression. I need that figure in my life who wants me to succeed not only in my career but emotionally and mentally. I miss you every day and I'm sorry I don't express that more.
